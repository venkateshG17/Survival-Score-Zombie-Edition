Welcome to the Zombie Apocalypse Survival Test

About This Test

The Zombie Apocalypse Survival Test is designed to assess your chances of surviving in a world overrun by zombies. It evaluates your readiness across several important categories, including:

  • Scenario-Based Questions: Test how you handle critical situations and make decisions in high-pressure survival scenarios.
  • Physical Tasks: Gauge your physical abilities, such as running, stamina, and handling different survival skills.
  • Medical Health: Assess your current medical condition and how it might impact your ability to survive.

This test is based on your personal opinions and decisions — there are no correct or wrong answers. It simply gives you an idea of how well-prepared you might be for a zombie apocalypse, based on your responses.

Remember, survival is not just about physical strength but also about quick thinking, problem-solving, and adapting to the situation at hand. Good luck!

Choose Your Role

Before taking the test, you can select a role that best fits your personality and skills. Each role offers a different approach to surviving the zombie apocalypse:

Gym Guy

The Gym Guy is all about fitness. He’s strong, has high endurance, and is ready to take on zombies physically. If you're into fitness and believe your strength and stamina will help you survive, this is your role.

Athletic Guy

The Athletic Guy has excellent speed, agility, and reflexes. Whether it’s sprinting from danger or jumping over obstacles, this role is perfect if you think you can outrun and outmaneuver zombies in tough situations.

Nerd Guy

The Nerd Guy relies on intelligence and strategy over physical power. He’s great at solving problems, hacking systems, and using his smarts to find solutions. If you believe brains will outwit brawn in the apocalypse, this role is for you.

Noob Guy

The Noob Guy is just starting out and may not have the experience, skills, or confidence to survive. But with the right decisions, he might just learn on the go and make it through. Choose this role if you’re unsure but ready to give it a shot.
